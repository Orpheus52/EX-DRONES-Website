What is EX-POWER?

EX-POWER is the official album for EX-DRONES which was released on the 16th of March 2022. The Album added ambience and electro beats for the game and it was added to the game!










What is EX-POWER Anniversary Edition?

EX-POWER Anniversary Edition OR 1st Anniversary Edition brought 2 EXTRA Tracks, Remastered Sound, and Enchanced Audio. Of course, as a typical Anniversary, EX-POWER Anniversary Edition was released the next year on the 16th of March 2023! The album was released before NEXT-GEN was even in the works, we took that as an advantage and a huge opportunity for the NEXT-GEN and added the full album into the games' Menu. Electro beats now extra peachy!.

Where can i buy the album or listen to it?

In case you want to support us and Had Funk, we will provide you the suitable links down below to the website where you can buy the album! Noneless, you can listen to both of the albums for free on YouTube!










Will i get EX-POWER by purchasing EX-DRONES?

EX-POWER + 1st Anniversary Edition are both included in the EX-EDITION of the game so, by buying the EX-EDITION you get the full EX-POWER experience in 1 pack! We call it, All In One.










Who is Had Funk?

Had Funk is the artist behind EX-POWER. EX-POWER is supposedly to be their 2nd album release! We recommend you checking their music out! Visit their Itch.io! or their YouTube!
