"Is NEXT-GEN a free upgrade?"

If you already owned "EX-Drones" before then yes, this is just a free update for you! If you previously didn't own EX-Drones, you can now buy it and play! With less words, for the owners of EX-Drones, you get NEXT-GEN for FREE!





"What is PORTAL?"

PORTAL is the new EX-DRONES huge Gamemode which comes with the NEXT-GEN update. PORTAL offers you the ability to play as the 3 Operators, the legendary character "EX-Guy", the bravest wingman "Stewart" and the anarchic bandit "Kyle". Those 3 operators can offer you a different innovative gameplay each character is unique, with different lore that makes the game more interesting and the characters more alive.
